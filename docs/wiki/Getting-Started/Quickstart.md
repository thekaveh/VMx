# Quickstart

The smallest VMx shape is the same in every language flavor:

1. create a `MessageHub`
1. create a foreground-capable dispatcher
1. build leaf `ComponentVM` instances
1. compose them under a container such as `CompositeVM`
1. construct the tree and drive selection through the VM surface
