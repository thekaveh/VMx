# Capability Families

Capabilities let consumers depend on what a VM can do rather than on its
concrete type.

## Capability Groups

- selection and expansion verbs
- lifecycle verbs
- dialog and form verbs
- search, filter, and paging
- CRUD and current-item CRUD

## Guidance

Capabilities are additive contracts. They do not replace the core hierarchy or
reshape ownership.
